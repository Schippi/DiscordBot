Jackbox Launcher

What is Jackbox Launcher?

It displays all the different Jackbox games, so you can launch the Pack the game is in without needing to remember which Pack it is in.

How do I use Jackbox Launcher?

Just click on the game you want to launch, the correct Party-Pack will launch or Steam will ask you to install it.

What is a Steam ID?

A SteamID is a unique identifier used to identify a Steam account. It is also used to refer to a user's Steam Community profile page.

Why do you need my Steam ID?

To find out which of the games you have access to.

How do I find my Steam ID?

It is the 17 digit number in your profile URL. If you have a vanity URL use the following tool to find your Steam ID: Find your Steam ID
We specifically need the Steam64ID, which is the 17 digit number in your profile Permalink
